4th 4DIAC Users' Workshop

The 4th Users' Workshop has been hold in conjunction with the 19th IEEE International Conference on Emerging Technologies and Factory Automation (ETFA) in Cagliari, Italy.

Workshop Date: 10th September 2013

Workshop Organizers:

  • Alois Zoitl, fortiss GmbH
  • Gerhard Ebenhofer, PROFACTOR GmbH
  • Thomas Strasser, AIT Austrian Institute of Technology

Workshop Program:

Morning
Sessions 		Welcome and Recent Activities of the 4DIAC Open Source Initiative
G. Ebenhofer (Profactor GmbH), A. Zoitl (fortiss GmbH)
Application

F. Andrén and T. Strasser (Austrian Institute of Technology)
4DIAC integration into the FASA project: a success story of increased maintainability and modularity
V. Domova*, E. Ferranti+, T. de Gooijer*, A. Vulgarakis* (*ABB Corporate Research, Industrial Control Systems, Västerås, Sweden; +ABB Corporate Research, Industrial Control Systems, Baden-Dättwil, Switzerland)

Afternoon
Hands on
Session

Programming Distributed Embedded Real-Time Control Systems with 4DIAC

In the afternoon the 4DIAC team explained how to use 4DIAC. The following topics have been covered:

  • Short introduction to IEC 61499
  • Overview on 4DIAC-IDE
  • Guided small control application example: Blinken Lights
  • The example project with the final application can be downloaded from here (use import existing project into workspace to get it into 4DIAC-IDE)

The 4DIAC team wants to thank all presenters and attendees for this interesting and inspiring workshop!
